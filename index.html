About Us

Vrinda Girls' Hostel is one of the safest, most comfortable and the best girls' hostels in ujjain. A brand new hostel building awaits you at indra nagar, agar road. It's only a 1 km walking distance from Govt. Dhanwantari Ayurved Medical College & Hospital mangalnath road that makes it easier for girls to commute. We understand that most of our students are moving away from home for the first time, so we strive to make the transition as smooth as possible by providing comfortable, safe and homely set-up within the property. All the rooms have been designed to provide adequate moving space and ample air and light.

Vision

Our vision to raise standards of hostel accommodation in the city.

Mission

Our Mission to offer home-like accommodation to students who live away from their homes.

Want to change

We want to be recognized as a name that changed the way hostels are perceived in today’s time. We have blended the charms of old-school hostel discipline with modern facilities without losing the comfort and luxury factors. Vrinda Hostels have set a trend of luxury living coupled with a cozy environment.
